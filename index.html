Mantra Meditation & Prana{y}ama Workshop 10/13/18

In this workshop Jen will teach the pronunciation and meaning of 3 special yoga Mantras, the Gayatri Mantra, Maha Mrityunjaya, and a Peace Mantra focused on discernment, then we recite them together in Sanskrit, in a traditional counting fashion. 

Then we will learn some potent breathing techniques for anxiety, stress, lung health, and mental clarity. Meditating between each technique to feel the effects on the mind and body. Silent meditation will take place in between mantra and breathing practice.

Finishing the class with 2  Restorative Poses to soak in all the magic of the sound and breath work.


Please click here to prerigister. Drop in's welcome too!

The class is 90 min and the investment is $15, you will take home a booklet of the practices taught.


Lotus Palm Thai Massage Workshop 11/ 10/ 18

In this workshop we will learn the fun techniques of Lotus Palm Thai Yoga Massage. By giving and receiving massage in specific locations and switching back and forth from giver to recipient, we will move slowly and talk thouroughly through the techniques of a 90 minute massage. 

By working in a loving compassionate way, this becomes a dynamic practice for physical well being and spiritual energy.

Lotus Palm bodywork emphasizes centering, transition, balance, safety, and using the least amount of effort to achieve maximum results.

Join us and learn some techniques before the holidays, Massage is such a gift!


Please click here to pay in advance. Space is limited (12 total)

Investment: $30 - No partner required! You will take home the massage sequence taught.

 Monthly Moon Lodge at Athens Yoga
"red tent gathering"
October 28, 7-9

A moon lodge is simply a gathering of women (or anyone identifying as) that provides community, support and space. This is a timeless tradition observed by ancient cultures which serves as a regular forum for empowering women's sense of selfhood. In moon lodge, women's deepest secrets can be shared, so no woman has to carry her burdens alone.

Come gather and share tea, stories and space.

Bring your favorite mug and a blanket.

Event listed on our FB page, feel free to send us a message with any questions!

What is a cacao ceremony?

A ceremonial dose of 100% pure ceremonial grade cacao (1.5 oz) is served in the form of a warm drink. This heart opening elixir can facilitate a pleasant, perhaps blissful state where participants can journey deeper into personal and collective healing. Ceremonies may include sound healing, meditation, drumming, song, conscious art making, connection, sharing, dance, journeying and asana. This ancient medicine helps connect participants with their inner guide while it may increase clarity, focus, creativity and an overall sense of wellbeing.
