Prenatal/Postpartum Yoga
next workshop TBA


This workshop,and classes in the future will be  led by Ahley Wines, and   is for prenatal and postpartum mothers. Created to be a grounding place for mothers to gather and collect the mind & body through meditation,discussion, and yoga. We will discuss the transition from pregnancy to postnatal and effective ways to stay grounded into our yoga practice. We will explore how we can show up for ourselves in time of rest and nourishment on and off the Mat.   Motherhood is hard and by honoring that, we can honor ourselves through rest and cultivation amongst other wombyn. 

I believe there is no “time” limit on postpartum, if this calls to you please Come!


The mother in me, honors the mother in you. 

No yoga experience necessary.  Please bring notebook and pen •••

 Monthly Moon Lodge at Athens Yoga
"red tent gathering"
One Sunday per month 
7-9PM

A moon lodge is simply a gathering of women and non-bianry folks that provides community, support and space. This is a timeless tradition observed by ancient cultures which serves as a regular forum for empowering women's sense of selfhood. In moon lodge, women's deepest secrets can be shared, so no woman has to carry her burdens alone.

Come gather and share tea, stories and space.

Bring your favorite mug and a blanket.

Event listed on our FB page, feel free to send us a message with any questions!

FOUNDATIONS OF ASHTANGA YOGA
with Abi Lynne

----Ashtanga Foundations - In this workshop Abi will introduce the Ashtanga Yoga practice and history as taught by Pattahbi Jois, talk about the benefits and method of Ashtanga, and work through some of the signature movements and poses though the lens of the Tristana Method (breath, asana and pranayama). 

--- Primary Series Breakdown: In this workshop we will move through the poses of the Primary Series of Ashtanga Yoga. We will break down the elements of each pose (through Half-Primary), go over alignment, adjustments, and some tips for working the poses on your own, and talk about how each pose builds on each other and prepares you for what's to come. Prepare to move!

ABOUT: Abi Lynne Wright (E-RYT 200) is an Ashtanga Yoga teacher based out of Columbus, Ohio where she studies closely with Authorized Level II teacher, Taylor Hunt at Ashtanga Yoga Columbus.  Although she always balked at rules and structure, the method of Ashtanga Yoga provided a way for her to tune inward to find healing each morning.  She is passionate about the practice of Ashtanga Yoga, and believes is much more than just a series of poses to work through, it also provides a lifestyle that supports the whole person. As a teacher, she is passionate about sharing the practice in an approachable way that can work for each student. She also enjoys the platform of social media to reach those who otherwise wouldn't have access to teachers. Check her out and say hello on instagram @abilynneyoga
